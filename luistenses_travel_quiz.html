Grammar Reference

SIMPLE PRESENT

  • Habits & routines
  • General truths / facts
  • Feelings & states now
  • Schedules & timetables
  • Markers: always, usually, every day…

PRESENT PERFECT

  • Past actions with present relevance
  • Life experiences (ever / never)
  • Recent events (just / already / yet)
  • Unfinished time periods (today, this week)
  • Markers: already, just, yet, ever, never, since, for…
