Quick Reference

IN Longer periods: months, years, seasons, parts of the day (morning, afternoon, evening), centuries.
ON Specific days & dates: weekdays, weekends, holidays (Christmas Day, New Year's Day), specific dates.
AT Precise times & fixed points: clock times, night, noon, midnight, the weekend (BrE).
