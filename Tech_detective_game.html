Two Tech People —
I work vs. She works

Read the text · Notice the patterns · Answer the questions

1st person — I / We
3rd person — He / She / It
Part 1 — Read

Two Colleagues, Two Worlds

A SHORT TEXT · PRESENT SIMPLE · AFFIRMATIVE

My name is Marco. I work as a software developer in Montreal. Every day, I use Python to build small automation tools. I also love electronics — I design circuits with Arduino in my free time. At home, I have a son. He is young, but he already loves technology too.

I have a colleague at work. Her name is Sara. She works on the same team as me. Sara knows a lot about machine learning — she trains AI models and she writes Python scripts every day. She also uses cloud platforms like AWS. Sara reads a lot of research papers. She says that AI changes everything.

I agree with her. I think AI makes our work faster and smarter. At my company, a new AI assistant helps us write better code. It suggests solutions and it finds bugs automatically. I really enjoy using it.

Every Friday, Sara and I have a short meeting. She shares what she learns that week, and I show her my new circuit projects. I think she understands electronics better than me now!

📌 Notice the pattern

1st person — I

I work · I use · I think
→ verb stays the same

3rd person — She / He / It

She works · It finds · He trains
→ always add -s
Part 2 — Write your answers
1
1st person · about you
What do you do at work every day?
Write 1–2 sentences. Start with: I …
Write at least one sentence.
Example answer I work as a software developer. Every day, I write code and I test new tools.
2
1st person · about you
What technology do you use in your free time?
Write 1–2 sentences. Start with: I …
Write at least one sentence.
Example answer In my free time, I design circuits with Arduino. I also use AI tools to help me with my projects.
3
3rd person · about Sara
What does Sara do at work? Write 2 things.
Look at the text. Use: She …s
Write at least one sentence.
Example answer She trains AI models. She also writes Python scripts every day.
4
3rd person · about the AI tool
What does the AI assistant at Marco's company do?
Use: It …s
Write at least one sentence.
Example answer It helps the team write better code. It suggests solutions and it finds bugs automatically.
5
3rd person · about someone you know
Think of a person you know. What do they do with technology?
Write 2 sentences. Use their name or: He / She …s
Write at least one sentence.
Example answer My son loves video games. He plays every evening and he knows a lot about computers.
6
1st + 3rd person · compare
Compare yourself with Sara. Write 2 sentences — one about you, one about her.
Use: I … and She …s
Write at least two sentences.
Example answer I design circuits and I use Arduino. Sara trains AI models and she uses cloud platforms.
★
Bonus · Free writing
Describe an AI tool you know. What does it do? Write 3 sentences.
Think of ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Midjourney… Use: It …s
Write at least 2 sentences.
Example answer I use Claude every day. It generates text and it answers questions. It also helps me write better emails in English.
