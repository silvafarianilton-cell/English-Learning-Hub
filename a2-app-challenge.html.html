Grammar Challenge
Present Simple & Past Simple — choose the right form
Present Simple → things that are always true or happen regularly.
I use Instagram every day. / She downloads apps on her phone.

Past Simple → things that happened and finished in the past.
I downloaded TikTok last week. / He sent a message yesterday.
