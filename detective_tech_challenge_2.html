Grammar — Present Simple
Choose the correct verb form for each sentence.
Present Simple — Key Rules
I / you / we / they → base verb  →  I use, they send, we check
he / she / it → verb + s / es  →  she uses, he watches, it works
Negative:  I don't use  /  she doesn't use

1. Every day, I use my phone to _______________
💡 Use: present simple (I) + an app activity  →  check, send, watch, listen, play, download
✦ MODEL ANSWER
Every day, I use my phone to check my messages and listen to music.
Other good answers: "watch videos", "send voice notes", "play games", "scroll through Instagram"
✔ Check your answer: Did you use the base verb after "to"?  (to check, to listen — no -s needed here!)
2. My best friend _______________ every day on his / her phone.
💡 Use: present simple (he / she) → verb + s  →  watches, sends, plays, uses, checks
✦ MODEL ANSWER
My best friend watches videos on YouTube every day on his phone.
My best friend sends voice notes on WhatsApp every day on her phone.
Other good answers: "plays games", "checks Instagram", "uses TikTok"
✔ Check your answer: Did you add -s to the verb?  (he/she → watches, sends, plays)
3. My friends and I _______________ when we are bored.
💡 Use: present simple (we) — no -s!  →  watch, play, send, scroll, use, listen
✦ MODEL ANSWER
My friends and I watch videos on YouTube when we are bored.
My friends and I play games on our phones when we are bored.
Other good answers: "scroll through Instagram", "send funny memes", "listen to music"
✔ Check your answer: "My friends and I" = we → no -s on the verb!  (watch, play — not watches/plays)
4. My mum / dad doesn't use _______________ because _______________
💡 Name an app + give a reason  →  he/she doesn't + base verb  →  it is difficult / she prefers / he doesn't like
✦ MODEL ANSWER
My mum doesn't use TikTok because she doesn't like short videos.
My dad doesn't use Instagram because he prefers WhatsApp.
Note: after "doesn't", the verb has no -s → doesn't use (not uses)
✔ Check your answer: After doesn't, did you use the base verb with no -s?  (doesn't use ✓ / doesn't uses ✗)
